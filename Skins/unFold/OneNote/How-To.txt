Open the Photoshop File
Change the Text to suit your program
Place your Icon in the middle of the Crosshair, Icon size: 150px

Save as desired name and as png in @Resources\Buttons\Black
Invert the whole image and save as the same name and as png in @Resources\Buttons\White

In the .ini: Replace
NAMEHERE with the name of your button/program
PATHHERE with the path of your program
